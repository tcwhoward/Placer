Our Story

I'm never going to find my dream home at this rate...

 

Its a familiar frustration felt by househunters as they spend hours browsing the same old websites, looking at the smae properties over and over again in the hope that they'll come across a new one that's just hit the market in exactly the right area. What we really want is just to know who is interested in selling at a price we can afford in the area we want to live.

 

It was also a familiar story for both Michael and Tim, the founders of Placer and recent househunters. Until, that is, they heard another story that really got them thinking. Their friend Andrew had also been in the market recently and experienced exactly the same frustration but instead of just sitting back he took matters into his own hands.

 

Andrew knew where he wanted to live so decided to write a personal letter about his family and his desire to live in the areas, including contact details for anyone interested in selling. Printing 1,000 copies he letter-dropped his favourite streets one weekend. With a little bit of surprise a number of emails started arriving that next week and within the month he negotiated and completed the purchase of his dream home.

 

It turns out the owner was thinking of selling but had held off because she didn't have the time to commit to a lengthy process but when the opporunity came knocking it removed that hassle and made it so much easier.

 

Michael and Tim understood the power of a well told story and could see the opportunity that this little test by Andrew had validated. So Placer was founded, with a missino to empower Buyers and make life easy for Owners.
